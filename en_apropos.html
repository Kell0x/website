My career

My actual situation

Today I'm still employed by Orange CyberDefense, since September 2020. I'm working as a Threat & Vulnerabilities Management expert.

ESIEA Laval, 2015 - 2020

CTI accredited engineering degree

During my second year at ESIEA, I realized with my team a functional drone, which we made the plans ourselves. The goal of the project was to take advantage of a 3D printer that was used to print most of the parts, but also to look into the complex electronics of this kind of airborne systems.

During my third year at ESIEA, I participated, within a team of students managed by a teacher from the security lab, in the development of an automated web auditing platform, mixing Python and PHP. The platform was intended to offer a first non-exhaustive vision of the security of a site, all for free.

My fourth year, shorter by a semester that I spent studying in Thailand, allowed me to work on a "VR ROOM" concept, a three-dimensional simulation of an environment fitted out with various equipment to offer an interactive experience to a user, while offering a haptic feedback approach at the scale of a room of about 12m².

Orange CyberDefense, 2019 - 2020

Internship in the pentest team of Cesson-Sévigné, in Brittany.
I designed a pivoting tool in Golang which was used as a subject for my thesis.
I was also able to create a challenge for the Hack 2020, before this edition was cancelled.
In parallel, I participated in security audit missions with the team.

Orange CyberDefense, 2019

Internship in the pentest team of Cesson-Sévigné, in Brittany.
During my internship, I designed a tool to facilitate the deployment of a team on a site for an audit. This tool, written in Python and working with Docker, allows to generate a disposable operating system and embedding the necessary tools for the good progress of the mission.

KMUTT (Bangkok), 2018

During this study semester abroad, I participated in the redesign and modernization of the mission reporting system for the local company DCS. The developed application works with Node.JS.
